Instant Tab Recorder

Blazing simple tab recorder.

Add to Chrome

Features

One-Click Recording

Start recording any Chrome tab instantly with a single click. No complicated setup required.

Context Menu Support

Right-click to start or stop recording. Perfect for popup windows and special tabs.

Customizable Settings

Configure video resolution, bitrate, and output format (WebM/MP4) to match your needs.

Microphone Recording

Optionally include microphone audio to add narration or commentary to your recordings.

Cropping Area

Define a specific area to record before starting.

Video or Audio Only

Record video only, audio only, or both. Flexible options for any use case.

Window Resize

Resize the browser window to a specific dimension before recording for consistent output.

Works Offline

No internet connection required. All recording and processing happens locally on your device.

How to Use

1

Install the Extension

Add Instant Tab Recorder to Chrome from the Chrome Web Store.

2

Click to Record

Click the extension icon or right-click and select "Start Recording" to begin.

3

Save & Share

Stop the recording and download your video in WebM or MP4 format.
